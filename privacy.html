Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information GreenSage collects, how we use it, and what choices you have.

GreenSage Last Updated: February 9, 2026

GreenSage ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at greensage.pages.dev (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our Site and use our free solar savings calculator, EV comparison tool, carbon footprint calculator, and related services.

By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the Site.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Voluntarily Provide

  • Email Address: If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter or provide your email through any form on the Site, we collect your email address. Providing your email is entirely voluntary and is not required to use our calculators or browse the Site.

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Calculator Data: When you use our solar, EV, or carbon footprint calculators, the inputs and results (such as electricity bill amounts, vehicle details, and energy usage estimates) are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • User Preferences: Settings such as your selected theme, preferred units, or dismissed notifications are stored in localStorage on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Third-party services integrated into our Site (specifically Google AdSense) may place cookies on your device to serve personalized advertisements and measure ad performance. See Section 4 for details.
  • Log Data: Our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) may collect standard web server log data, including your IP address, browser type, referring page, and the date and time of your visit. This data is collected and managed by Cloudflare under their own privacy policy.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require user accounts, passwords, or login credentials.
  • We do not collect payment information or process financial transactions.
  • We do not collect your precise geolocation.
  • We do not collect Social Security numbers or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

Information Purpose
Email address (if provided) To send newsletters, updates about new calculators or features, and relevant green living content. You can unsubscribe at any time.
localStorage data To save your calculator inputs and results so they persist between visits, improving your experience. This data stays on your device.
Cookies (via third parties) To serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense and to measure ad performance.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

GreenSage itself does not set cookies. However, the third-party services described above (primarily Google AdSense) may place cookies on your device. These cookies fall into the following categories:

Cookie Type Purpose Set By
Advertising cookies Serve relevant ads and measure ad performance Google AdSense / Google Ad Partners
Analytics cookies Understand how visitors interact with ads Google
Security cookies Protect against abuse and ensure content delivery Cloudflare

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually or in bulk.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific sites.
  • Block all cookies entirely.

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of some features on the Site and other websites you visit.

localStorage

In addition to cookies, we use your browser's localStorage to save calculator data and user preferences. Unlike cookies, localStorage data is never sent to any server automatically. You can clear localStorage data at any time through your browser's developer tools or site settings. Clearing your browser data or localStorage will reset all saved calculator inputs and preferences.

  • localStorage data: Calculator data and preferences stored in localStorage persist on your device until you manually clear them or clear your browser data. We have no control over the retention of this data, as it resides entirely on your device.
  • Third-party data: Data collected by Google AdSense, Amazon, EnergySage, and Cloudflare is retained according to their respective privacy policies and data retention schedules.
